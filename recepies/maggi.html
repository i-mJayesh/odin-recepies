MAGGI

Description

Maggi is a noddle based food with a spicy flavour. It could be said as the indian version of ramen.

Ingredients

  • Packet of maggi
  • water
  • salt(if needed)

Steps

  1. Start boiling 1 and half cup water.
  2. When water comes to boiing point mix the masala
  3. add the noodle to the hot water
  4. Let the noodle soak the masala water
